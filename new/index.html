What is WaterScrumFall?

WaterScrumFall -- /wa-ter scrum-fall/ -- noun
1. A mutation of the Waterfall and Scrum processes; considered harmful

Symptom 1

Two week engineering cycle, but Customers get new software quarterly.

Symptom 2

Product requirements something something

Symptom 3

Incremental releases, but no change to backlog after each release
